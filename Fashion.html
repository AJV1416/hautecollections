Bella Hadid Shows Why a Summer Cardigan Is a Style Win

Yesterday, the model went out in New York City wearing the perfect summer-night ensemble. She knows the power of a good cardigan, even in a season where it may not seem an obvious first choice. Meet the New It Bag Set to Dominate New York Fashion Week

First seen at Gucci’s Resort 2020 show, the Italian label is launching the 1955 Horsebit handbag this month, a new style that references Gucci’s archives from the ’50s. The revived interpretation features the brand’s iconic horsebit clasp, the first time the signature detail has been used on a bag since Alessandro Michele took the helm in 2015.

SZA Brings Sustainable Fashion to Festival Style

Since the singer-songwriter announced the launch of Ctrl Fishing Company—a brand committed to saving the ocean, as she framed it in an Instagram story last year—she has debuted numerous different pieces from the collection. She gave us an initial peek at the upcoming line this past December with a colorful, ’80s-inspired windbreaker with the words “Puck Flastic” embroidered onto it in bold lettering, and just a few months ago, she showed off one of Ctrl Fishing Company’s upcycled shirts, patched together from old tie dye tees.
