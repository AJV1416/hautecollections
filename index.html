About

Our inspiration for our website came from the different point of views we all had when we discussed what we envisioned, we didn't want to exclude anyone’s ideas so we wanted to create a website that was going to merge our ideas together. With that we came up with three topics that we each were passionate about Stephanie was interested in fashion, Isabella wanted to spread a more healthy lifestyle, and Alondra wanted bring awareness to people on what is going on around in the world. We were first inspired by a few websites like Teen Vogue and Vogue, then after that we started to think of what we wanted to include on each of the pages. We then separated into our own topic and started a rough draft of what was going to be included in each web page.One of the main reason why stephanie wanted to choose fashion was because “Fashion can be used to communicate a message on a larger scale, for example the intersection of fashion and social movements in a comprehensive way. Fashion has always been a powerful political tool used to communicate a message, positive or negative, like the suffragettes and the Black Panthers”. Isabella was leaning more on the food/cooking side and she was able to share a variety of meals that can be made at home and that are also more nutritious. Alondra at the beginning wanted to shed light on the deportations that have recently been happening but she thought that making a main page that talked about news all around would be more beneficial because then people would be globally informed.
